How it works

Create. Post. Get Candidates.

Auto Apply helps identify the ideal candidate through a simplified process by providing step-by-step guidance throughout the candidate submission process.

  • Create Job

    Indentify the desired criteria of the open role that should be used to automatically filter applications.

  • Auto Apply

    Auto Apply provides thorough screening of potential candidates to guarantee they meet the necessary requirements for the role.

  • Candidate Received

    Only qualified applicants who have met the selected criteria are accepted into the ATS.

Big things come in small packages.

  • Keep Track of Candidates

    It's simple to keep tabs on and manage potential candidates for each job offer, or get an overview of everyone who has applied to all available jobs.

  • Candidate Tracking Pipeline

    Get a bird's-eye view of the job's candidate pipeline and effortlessly supervise potential applicants in the recruitment process.
